About

So who are you again?
Hi! I’m Nick, an Aussie web developer from Adelaide. I like to create new and awesome web content, and love the challenge of crafting it all.

And how’d you get here?
Well my friend, I started my developer life by acquiring a Bachelor in Digital Media, followed with 6 months of front-end developer work experience before being hired as a junior web developer at Talent2 creating e-learning websites, where I worked for 2 years learning and building up my skillset. Upon finishing my 2nd year at Talent2, I did the sensible thing of ditching everything to go travelling! I spent the next 6 months travelling around Europe, seeing awesome things and meeting awesome people. Then once my travel ended I did another sensible thing and re-located to the wonderful city of Cardiff.

Uh... so... do you like... stuff?
Sure do mate! I have a big passion for gaming. I have an embarrassingly large steam list, and a slowly growing boardgame collection. I also thoroughly enjoy watching and playing American football. I was part of the Oilers Gridiron team back in Adelaide and was even fortunate enough to win an AllStar award for OLB. I’ve also recently discovered the show The Chase, and holy moly, am I addicted.
